Is It Safe and Legal to Use?

Downloading is safe from a technical standpoint: a browser-based tool like this doesn't need account access, passwords, or app permissions, since it only reads the public video link that's pasted in. The bigger question is usage rights. TikTok videos still belong to the creator who posted them, even after they're downloaded. Saving a clip for private viewing, backup, or personal editing practice is generally fine, but reposting someone else's content publicly without credit or permission can run into copyright and platform rules. The safest habit is to download for personal use, and to tag or credit the original creator any time a saved clip gets shared elsewhere.
